Phy112 Equations

General

\[\sum \vec{F} = m \vec{a}\]

\[F_{\textnormal{centripital}} = \frac{m v^2}{r}\]

Electric Fields

\[F = k_e \frac{|q_1| |q_2|}{r^2}\]

\[\vec{E}= \frac{\vec{F}}{q}\]

\[E = k_e \frac{q}{r^2}~~~~\leftarrow \textnormal{point charge}\]

\[E = \frac{\sigma}{2 \epsilon_0} ~~~~\leftarrow \textnormal{charged plane}\]

\[\Phi_E = EA = \frac{Q_{in}}{\epsilon_0}\]

Electric Potential

\[\Delta PE = - W_{AB} = -q E_x \Delta x\]

\[\Delta V = - E_x \Delta x\]

\[V = k_e \frac{q}{r}\]

\[PE = k_e \frac{q_1 \cdot q_2}{r}\]

\[k_e = 8.99 \cdot 10^9 ~\frac{N \cdot m^2}{C}\]

\[\epsilon_0 = \frac{1}{4\pi k_e} = 8.85 \cdot 10^{-12} ~ \frac{C^2}{N\cdot m^2}\]

Capacitors

\[Q = C~ \Delta V\]

Current and Resistance

\[\Delta V = I ~R\]

\[R = \rho \frac{\ell}{A}\]

\[\mathcal{P} = I \Delta V\]

RC circuits

\[\tau = R C\]

\[q = Q(1-e^{-t/\tau})~~~~\leftarrow \textnormal{charging}\]

\[\Delta V = \mathcal{E} (1-e^{-t/\tau})~~~~\leftarrow \textnormal{charging}\]

\[q = Qe^{-t/\tau}~~~~\leftarrow \textnormal{discharging}\]

\[\Delta V = \mathcal{E} e^{-t/\tau}~~~~\leftarrow \textnormal{ discharging}\]

Magnetism

\[F = qvB \sin(\theta) = \ell I B \sin(\theta)\]

\[r=\frac{mv}{qB}\]

\[\mu_0 = 4\pi*10^{-7}~H/m\]

\[B = \frac{\mu I }{2 \pi r} \qquad \leftarrow \textnormal{Current line}\]

\[B = \mu \frac{N }{\ell} I \qquad \leftarrow \textnormal{Solenoid}\]

\[\Phi_B = B A \cos(\theta)\]

\[\mathcal{E} = - N\frac{\Delta \Phi_B}{\Delta t}\]

\[\mathcal{E} = - L\frac{\Delta I}{\Delta t}\]

\[L = \frac{N \Phi_B}{I}\]

\[I = \frac{\mathcal{E}}{R} \left( 1 - e^{-t/\tau} \right)\]

\[\tau = L/R\]

Optics

\[n_1 \sin(\theta_1) = n_2 \sin(\theta_2)\]

\[\sin(\theta_c) = \frac{n_2}{n_1}\]

\[M = -q/p\]

\[\frac1f = \frac1p + \frac1q\]

\[d\, \sin(\theta) = m \lambda\]

Relativity

\[c= \frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu_0\,\varepsilon_0}} = \lambda \, f\]

\[c= 299792458 m/s \approx 3e8 \, m/s\]

\[\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}}\]

\[L = \frac{L_0}{\gamma} \qquad \Delta t = \gamma \Delta t_0\]

\[E_\textnormal{rest} = mc^2 \qquad KE_{\textnormal{rel}} = (\gamma -1) mc^2\]

\[E^2 = (pc)^2 + (mc^2)^2\]

Radioactivity

\[E=hf=\frac{hc}{\lambda}\]

\[\Delta x \Delta p = \frac{h}{4\pi}\]

\[R = \frac{\Delta N}{\Delta t}\]

\[N = N_0 e^{-\lambda\,t}\]
